Due to the nature of digital services, all payments made are non-refundable once work has commenced. This includes, but is not limited to, campaign setup, creative work, audience research, optimization, and any communication or strategy provided.

2. What "Work Commenced" Means

Work is considered commenced as soon as any of the following occurs:

From this point forward, no refund will be issued regardless of campaign performance or early termination by the client.

3. Ad Spend Is Non-Refundable Through Us

Ad spend is paid directly by you to Meta. We do not collect, hold, or manage your ad budget. Any issues with ad spend are solely between you and Meta — we are not liable for refunds on ad spend under any circumstances.

4. Exceptions — When a Refund May Be Considered

A refund will only be considered in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service has not started — Payment was made but no onboarding, strategy, or setup work has been initiated by our team

Refund requests must be submitted in writing to clientnessmedia@gmail.com within 7 days of the issue arising. Requests submitted after this window will not be considered.

5. No Refunds Based on Results

Refunds will not be issued on the basis of dissatisfaction with campaign results, number of leads, sales, or ROAS. As stated in our Terms of Service, we do not guarantee specific outcomes from advertising.

6. Cancellation & Billing Cycle

For refund-related queries, contact us at clientnessmedia@gmail.com or book a call to discuss directly.
